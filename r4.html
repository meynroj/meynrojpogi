Buwan ng Wikang Pambansa

(Tagalog for 'National Language Month'), simply known as Buwan ng Wika ('Language Month'), is a month-long annual observance in the Philippines held every August to promote the national language, Filipino.

Supreme Student Government (SSG)

An SSG is a partnership between schools, parents/careers, the student and relevant agencies. The group works together to plan and support the educational, health, social, cultural and emotional well being of students with diverse learning needs.

Earthquake Drill

An earthquake drill is one set of simulated emergency circumstances designed to test the ability of school members to make the most appropriate decisions to ensure their safety. The school members should understand the emergency response plan sufficiently in order to react appropriately during the drill.

Worlds Teacher's Day

The Department of Education (DepEd) will lead this year’s World Teachers’ Day (WTD) celebration on October 5 with theme, “My Teacher, My Hero.” The venue of the celebration is at the Philippine Sports Arena (Ultra) in Pasig City where hundreds of Filipino teachers are expected to attend.

Year End Party

Year end parties are organized by students to celebrate the end of the year and to thank teacher for their hard work during the year. Some are organized by families or friends to celebrate the holiday season together. Some parties are organized by schools, community centers, or religious organizations to bring people together and to celebrate the holiday spirit.
